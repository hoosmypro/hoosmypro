Prof. Ochs and Prof. Misliuk:"I am part of the Creative Processes forum and this class is our required class for the first semester. I really love the nature of the class. We had many fun activities, such as dancing together, acting, walking around the classroom in different speeds, and making weird poses. We had guest speakers for every class. As for the workload, basically you need to write a response every week, reflecting on what you have learned in class and connecting it to the old materials. There is a 5-page midterm paper on what you have learned so far. The final is similar. Since it is a ""creative"" class, I guess everyone got an A in the end."
